WE'RE HERE TO HELP YOU

Order Tracking

How do I track my order once it has been shipped?
Once you’ve received your tracking number, you can track your order by visiting https://www.17track.net/en/track.

Shipping Information

How long does it take to get my order?
Please allow us up to 30 days for shipping and delivery from the date of your order.

Return Policy

What is your return policy?
We only allow returns within 30 days after the delivery of your order. If more than 30 days have gone by since your delivery, unfortunately we cannot offer you an exchange or price equivalent in store credit. For more details, please visit our Terms and Conditions.

What is your exchange policy?
For an exchange, we require a receipt or proof of purchase. Once your return is received and inspected by our customer service center, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your return.

Approved returns may be exchanged or issued a store credit equivalent to the purchase price. If you need to exchange your purchase for the same item, please contact customer service.

Product Information

Where can I find information about how my product works?
A product user guide comes with the product in the shipping package. If you have additional questions regarding how to use the product, please contact our customer service team and we will be glad to assist you further.

Lifetime Warranty

I didn’t purchase the lifetime warranty. What does this mean for me?
Unfortunately, we do not offer any repairs or replacement service without the purchase of the lifetime warranty.

Lifetime Warranty

What does my lifetime warranty cover?
If you purchased lifetime warranty, products and accessories contained in the original packaging are covered against defects in materials and workmanship when used normally in accordance with our published guidelines. Once the replacement request is submitted to our customer service team with the proof of purchase details, our team will assess the eligibility of the replacement and will assist your product replacement accordingly.
