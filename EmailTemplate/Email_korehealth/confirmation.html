Shipping Information
How long does it take to get my order?
Please allow up to 30 days for shipping and delivery from the date of your order.

Return Policy
What is your return policy?
We only allow returns within 30 days after the delivery of your order. Unfortunately, if more than 30 days have gone by since your delivery, we cannot offer you an exchange or price equivalent in-store credit. For more details, please visit our Terms and Conditions.

What is your exchange policy?
For an exchange, we require a receipt or proof of purchase. Once your return is received and inspected by our customer service center, we will send you a confirmation email. You will be notified about the approval or rejection of your return.

Approved returns may be exchanged or issued store credit equivalent to the purchase price. If you need to exchange your purchase for the same item, please contact customer service.
